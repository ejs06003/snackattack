Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2025

Introduction

Snack Attack ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game.

Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Unique user ID (automatically generated)

Game Data:

  • Game progress (points, rank, achievements)
  • Character selections and unlocks
  • Food inventory and perk usage
  • Gameplay actions (throws, hits, challenges completed)
  • Leaderboard rankings

Technical Information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and error reports (to improve app stability)
  • Push notification tokens (to send game notifications)

We DO NOT collect:

  • Your real name
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Payment information (handled securely by Apple)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Display your username and character on leaderboards
  • Save your game progress across devices
  • Send push notifications about game events (with your permission)
  • Detect and prevent cheating or abuse
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Process in-app purchases (via Apple)

Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored securely using Supabase (a PostgreSQL database service). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information.

Your data is shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • Public Leaderboards: Your username, character, rank, and points are visible to other players
  • Service Providers: Supabase (database hosting) and Apple (in-app purchases and push notifications)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Children's Privacy

Snack Attack is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@example.com and we will delete it.

In-App Purchases

All in-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle payment data.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications about:

  • Being attacked by other players
  • Daily challenge availability
  • Achievement unlocks
  • Leaderboard position changes

You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at support@example.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of push notifications
  • Export your game data

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@example.com.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and backend services (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple App Store: In-app purchases and distribution (Privacy Policy)

Analytics and Advertising

We do NOT use third-party analytics or advertising networks. All gameplay tracking is done internally to improve the game experience.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

International Users

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using Snack Attack, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell your data)
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@example.com
  • Support: support@example.com

Consent

By using Snack Attack, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
