Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?
Select a target player, choose your food weapon, pick how many to throw, and launch! Hit your target to earn points and climb the leaderboard. Miss and you lose your food with no points gained.
How do I earn points?
You earn points by successfully hitting other players with food. Hitting top players gives you bonus multipliers. Revenge throws also earn extra points!
What happens when I get hit?
When another player hits you, you lose points based on the damage dealt. You'll get a notification and can tap "Revenge" to strike back with bonus multipliers!
How do I unlock new characters?
Characters can be unlocked in three ways: (1) Free starter characters, (2) Purchase with in-game points in the Character Shop, (3) Complete achievements, or (4) Purchase premium packs.
What are Perks?
Perks are power-ups you can buy with points to give you advantages. They include accuracy boosts, damage multipliers, shields, and more. Use them strategically!
What happens if I run out of points?
You can always get free peas to keep playing. Complete daily challenges to earn bonus points, or purchase food with higher accuracy to reduce risk.
How do Daily Challenges work?
Three new challenges appear every day. Complete them to earn bonus points. Finish all three to unlock extra rewards!
What's the difference between food types?
Each food has different stats: accuracy (% chance to hit), damage (points dealt), and cost (points spent). Higher accuracy foods are safer but do less damage. Higher damage foods are riskier but earn more points.
How do I get to #1 on the leaderboard?
Earn more points than everyone else! Hit top players for bonus multipliers, complete daily challenges, use revenge throws strategically, and manage your point economy wisely. Reaching #1 unlocks the exclusive prestige character!
Are in-app purchases required?
No! The entire game is free to play. In-app purchases only unlock exclusive cosmetic characters and badges‚Äîthey don't give you any gameplay advantages.
How do I restore my purchases?
Purchases are tied to your Apple ID and will automatically restore when you log in. If you're having issues, make sure you're using the same Apple ID you made the purchase with.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account and all associated data, visit your in-app profile and view the settings. Tap the Delete Account button. This cannot be undone.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us at support@example.com with:
  • Description of the bug
  • What you were doing when it happened
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Screenshots if possible
We appreciate your help making the game better!
Can I suggest new features?
Absolutely! We love hearing from players. Send your ideas to support@example.com and we'll consider them for future updates.
Is my data safe?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers. We don't sell your data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
